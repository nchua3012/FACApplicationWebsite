About Me

Hello! I’m Nick Chua. I'm originally from Brunei and now based in the UK. As a former architect, I’ve led several large-scale projects in London and allowed me to develop my skills in design, problem-solving, and project management. My architectural experience has given me a strong foundation in understanding user needs, attention to detail, and the ability to translate complex ideas into functional, meaningful spaces.

Why I Want To Be A Developer

I want to become a developer because I enjoy coding and problem-solving. Like architecture, web development allows me to create engaging, interactive experiences that people can connect with. I’m excited by the problem-solving aspect of coding, where I can apply my architectural mindset to tackle new challenges. I'm looking forward to learning more and building on the skills I've learned so far.

I’m eager to continue learning and growing as a developer. In my free time, I enjoy photography and visual storytelling.

What I've Learned So Far

Over the past few months, I've been learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I've built a portfolio website, a game and have used codewars and Codeacademy to practice and learn the fundamentals of coding.
